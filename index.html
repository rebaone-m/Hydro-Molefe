About Hydro Molefe

Hydro Molefe, born Thabang Molefe, is a Motswana singer-songwriter and musician based in Gaborone, Botswana. His healing music blends traditional Botswana sounds with smooth jazz and afro-contemporary styles. Inspired by folk, improvised, and instrumental music, Hydro's melodic style reflects his deep roots in traditional dance and percussion. His debut album, Dimapo, is an eight-song journey featuring instruments like marimba, setinkane, and African drums, aiming to bring peace and enlightenment through culturally rich music.

Born and raised in Old Naledi, a hub for Botswana's finest artists, Hydro follows in the footsteps of vocal trailblazers like Lekofi Sejeso, George Swabi, Stika Sola, Setampore, and Ratsie Sethako. His passion for folk music was kindled by his love of traditional dance and percussion, leading him to win numerous local talent shows, including President and constituency competitions.
